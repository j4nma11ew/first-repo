SVS SUMMER CAMP

SVS Summer Camp 2018 is an opportunity to the +2 Students who are aspiring for Higher Edducation in Science and technology.The main aim of this camp is to instill scientific temper in students through innovative programs of life skills, holistic living and science practicals. The camp also aims at fueling scientific curiosity and encouraging teamwork among youngsters.

Go Kart is an open wheel motor sport commonly perceived as the stepping stone to the higher ranks of motor sports.Go Kart at this camp has been designed with fun filled racing to provide thrilling experience to the participants with top most safety precautions.


Outbound Training at SVS Summer Camp 2018 revolves around activities designed to improve leadership, communication skills, planning, delegation,teamwork and motivation. Participants are divided into teams and assigned tasks or activities for completion in a specified time.It is fun filled learning process.
